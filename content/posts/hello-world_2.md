## Why Go?

I chose Go for this blog engine because:

1. **Fast compilation** - Go compiles very quickly
2. **Simple syntax** - Easy to read and maintain
3. **Great standard library** - Lots of built-in functionality
4. **Static binaries** - No runtime dependencies
